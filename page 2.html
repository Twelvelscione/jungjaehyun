FOREVER ONLY
Selamanya Bersama

"Forever Only" is a song recorded by South Korean singer Jaehyun. It was released on August 18, 2022, alongside an accompanying music video, as the fourth single of the NCT Lab project. The song was written by Jaehyun, Dewain Whitmore Jr., Ellie Suh, Micah Premnath, and producer Tido Nguyen. It is described as a guitar-driven R&B and pop track reminiscent of the 2000s. Inspired by the time Jaehyun spent in Milan, the lyrics express one's yearning for the person they love. It charted at number 120 on the Circle Digital Chart in South Korea, and at number 14 on the Billboard World Digital Song Sales chart in the United States. The song received positive reviews from critics, with praise for its composition and Jaehyun's vocal performance.

TRY AGAIN
Coba Lagi

The song is about a couple who have been through difficult times but still want to try again. The protagonist sings about how their expectations have grown with the time they have spent together, and even though they have had many disagreements, their heart still belongs to their partner. The song ends with the couple reassuring each other that they will be alright and they should try again.

HORIZON
Horizon

“Horizon” appears to be a manifestation of JAEHYUN’s personal feelings and reflections on life, transitions, and exploration. Inspired by the simple yet profound act of taking off on an airplane, the song delves into themes of breaking free from monotony, embracing the unexpected, and surrendering to the journey. It seems to be a metaphorical exploration of personal growth, self-awareness, and the continuous pursuit of one’s dreams and aspirations.
